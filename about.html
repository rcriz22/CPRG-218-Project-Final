About Me

Hi, my name is Raizel Criz Tayao. I was born in the Philippines in 1992 and recently moved to Canada in April 2024. Adjusting to a new life here has been a big adventure, but it has also sparked my creativity and passion for sharing stories.

I’ve always had a deep love for traveling and discovering new cuisines. To me, every destination has a story, and every dish holds a memory. Whether it’s wandering through vibrant markets or trying a family recipe passed down through generations, I find joy in every experience.

This blog is my creative space to share those moments with you. From hidden travel gems to recipes inspired by my journeys, I hope to bring a piece of the world to your screen and maybe even your kitchen.

When I’m not traveling or cooking, you’ll likely find me enjoying music, sketching new ideas, or planning my next adventure. Life is about exploring, and I’m excited to have you join me on this journey.

Let’s taste the world, one journey at a time!
